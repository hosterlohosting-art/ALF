Frequently Asked Questions

Take a look at some of the most common personal injury law questions for general information, then reach out to our team for guidance specific to your case.

Results depend on the unique facts of each case; past outcomes do not guarantee similar results.

Case value depends on medical expenses, lost income, pain and suffering, liability, available insurance, and the strength of the evidence.

Get medical care, call the police when needed, document the scene, collect witness information, and avoid giving recorded statements before speaking with an attorney.

Personal injury cases are typically handled on a contingency fee, which means attorney fees are paid from the recovery if compensation is obtained.

Timing depends on medical treatment, records, negotiations, insurance response, and whether litigation is needed. Some claims settle quickly, while complex cases take longer.
