Mission and Vision

Our

Purpose

To advocate for people who have been injured with excellence, integrity, and faith based conviction. We fight to maximize what our clients receive because seeking full justice for those who have been harmed is the right thing to do.

Our

Legacy

To be the rare law firm that proves lawyers can be both exceptional advocates and exceptional human beings. Guided by faith and driven by excellence, we aspire to set a new standard in our profession where clients are treated with honor, extraordinary care, and unreasonable hospitality.

Client Promise

At The Awad Law Firm, we measure success by what you take home - not us. If your case resolves before a lawsuit, you'll receive equal to or more than our legal fee, with your medical bills covered. We believe this is fair, and we serve every client with unreasonable hospitality.
