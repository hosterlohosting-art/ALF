COMMUNITY EVENT • DALTON, GA

5,000 Tacos for Dalton: Awad Law Firm Gives Back

Awad Law Firm was proud to take part in a community event in Dalton, Georgia where more than 5,000 tacos were served to local residents. The event brought neighbors together in a spirit of generosity, connection, and shared support — exactly the kind of community-first effort our firm is honored to be part of.

A Smooth and Safe Event, Thanks to the Dalton Police Department

A special thank you goes to the Dalton Police Department for assisting with traffic control and helping ensure the event ran smoothly and safely for every resident who attended. Their presence and professionalism made it possible for thousands of people to enjoy the event without disruption.

Why Community Engagement Matters

Events like this highlight the importance of community engagement and the power of people coming together to support one another. What began as a simple act of giving quickly became a reminder that meaningful change often starts with small gestures that bring people together. When a law firm, a police department, and thousands of local residents show up for each other, it demonstrates what community truly looks like.

Our Commitment to Dalton

Awad Law Firm maintains an office in Dalton, Georgia at 210 North Glenwood Ave., and our commitment to this community goes far beyond the courtroom. We remain dedicated to supporting Dalton and the surrounding area through continued outreach that promotes unity, generosity, and lasting positive impact.

If you or a loved one has been injured and need a Georgia personal injury attorney who truly understands your community, we are here for you.
