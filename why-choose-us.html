Our Methodology

The Five Steps To Starting Your Claim

We guide you seamlessly from initial medical treatment to the final successful disbursement of your settlement.

1

Treatment

Seeking immediate, comprehensive medical attention is our top priority. We focus on your physical wellness above all else.

2

Collecting Records

Our advanced operations team gathers all medical charts, bills, witness accounts, and police reports securely.

3

Offer of Compromise

We compile a bulletproof demand package summarizing your legal claim and presenting your personal story with clarity.

4

Insurance Negotiation

We stand firm against multi-billion dollar insurance firms, negotiating aggressively to extract the absolute maximum value.

5

Settlement & Disbursement

Upon final secure settlement approval, we swiftly resolve outstanding bills and disburse your recovery check directly.
